Business summary
Working title: StartupSetGo (student-side marketplace & school pilot program)

Problem: Students with good ideas lack a simple path to validate, access vendors and apply for grants. Schools lack structured startup programs.

Solution: A platform that collects student ideas, runs school pilots, connects founders to vetted vendors & mentors, and guides them to relevant government schemes and incubators.

Target users: Students (class 6–12 & college), school principals, mentors & micro-vendors who support MVP building.

Business model (early): Commission / referral fees from vendors for successful projects, mentoring subscription for schools, and later B2B partnerships with incubators/education boards.

Current stage & traction: Idea stage; 5 ideas collected via school campaign; 1 small school pilot completed; initial mentor contacts established.
